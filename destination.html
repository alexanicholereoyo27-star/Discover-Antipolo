🌊 Hinulugang Taktak Falls

📍 Taktak Road, Brgy. Dela Paz, Antipolo City

Source: Fun In The Philippines Website

💬 Description: Hinulugang Taktak is a beautiful 21-meter waterfall in Antipolo that was restored and reopened to the public in 2015. It's a fun spot to visit with friends or family, offering exciting activities like crossing hanging bridges 🌉, climbing spider web platforms 🕸, and trying out the wall-climbing area 🧗🏻‍♂️. If you just want to relax, you can rent a cozy cottage for ₱500 a day and take a refreshing swim for only ₱50. It's a great place to enjoy nature and discover one of Rizal's most famous waterfalls! 🌊

🎨 Pinto Art Museum

📍 Sierra Madre St, Grand Heights Subdivision, Antipolo, 1870 Rizal

Source: Tripadvisor Website

💬 Description: Founded in 2010 by Dr. Joven Cuanang, Pinto Art Museum is a must-visit destination in Antipolo 🏛️ that features a diverse collection of artworks, including paintings 🖼️, sculptures 🗿, installations 🧩, and mixed media pieces 🎭. The museum celebrates Filipino artists 🇵🇭 whose creations explore themes of modernism, spirituality, and history 🌅. Set within a peaceful two-hectare botanical garden 🌿🌺, its charming Santorini-inspired architecture 🏠💙 offers plenty of picture-perfect spots for your Instagram feed 📸✨.

⛪ Antipolo Cathedral

📍 P. Oliveros Street, Antipolo City

Source: Antipolo Cathedral Page

💬 Description: The Antipolo Cathedral, officially known as the International Shrine of Our Lady of Peace and Good Voyage, is one of the most important religious sites in the Philippines. 🙏 This beautiful church houses the revered image of the Nuestra Señora de la Paz y Buen Viaje (Our Lady of Peace and Good Voyage), which has been venerated by Filipinos for centuries. The cathedral is a popular pilgrimage site, especially during the month of May when thousands of devotees visit as part of the traditional "Alay Lakad" pilgrimage. Whether you're seeking spiritual solace or admiring the architecture, the Antipolo Cathedral offers a profound and moving experience. ✝️

⏰ Open Hours: Daily, 5:00 AM – 9:00 PM
